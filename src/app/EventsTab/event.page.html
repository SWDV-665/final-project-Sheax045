All Pints North July 27th

Join the brewers of Minnesota by the shores of Lake Superior at Bayfront Festival Park in Duluth, and celebrate craft beer 2oz at a time! All Pints North will feature beer from breweries around the region, live music and tasty food in an iconic setting. All Pints North Summer Brew Fest takes place rain or shine. All attendees must be 21+ years of age and must present their valid 21+ ID at the gate. Please sample responsibly and plan ahead for transportation.
Bayfront Festival Park
350 Harbor Dr, Duluth, MN 55802 All Pints Eve July 26th

The fourth annual All Pints North pre-party in the Sir Ben’s parking lot features live music by the Fish Heads, Mackie Brothers, the Velvets and the Crunchy Bunch. There will be specialty beers available from 20 breweries, plus outdoor food by the Rambler and Crank & Dasher and kombucha by Duluth Kombucha. Free parking is available in the St. Luke’s lot adjacent to Sir Ben’s.

704 W Railroad St, Duluth, MN 55802 Grandma's Marathon June 20th

Grandma's Marathon is an annual road race held each June in Duluth, Minnesota, in the United States. The course runs point-to-point from the city of Two Harbors on Scenic Route 61 and continues along Lake Superior into the city of Duluth. If you are not a runner, being a spectator is a great way to get involved in the Grandma’s Marathon weekend festivities, without having to run 26.2 miles.
